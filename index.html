Information about the 3D Collection

 

This collection is referred to the 3D documentation project of archaeological artefacts preserved inside the National Museum of Turkmenistan in Ashgabat carried out by CRAST and University of Turin. The 3D documentation was performed by using non invasive structured-light scanners.

 

How to open and explore 3D models:

1) Select the desired model depending on museum ID;

2) Click on 3D play logo

3) Use the mouse to navigate the model: left click + mouvement to rotate the model; mouse wheel to zoom it; right click + mouvement to shift the model;

4) If you prefer, you can visualize the 3D model in full screen mode by clicking the dedicated button in the bottom right-hand corner;

5) You can visualize the model also without the texture layer by clicking on Model Inspector and then Matcap. To reset the view click the Final Render mode.
